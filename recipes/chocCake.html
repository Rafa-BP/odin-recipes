Chocolate Cake

Description: A simple and fluffy chocolate cake, perfect for afternoon coffee. It can be served plain or with frosting, and its sweet aroma is irresistible.

    Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Directions: Blend all ingredients in a blender, starting with the liquids. Add the baking powder last and mix gently. Pour into a greased pan and bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for 35–40 minutes.
